Capabilities

Built for the questions vendors actually ask

Read answers instantly. Write operations are proposed, never executed, until you approve.

Natural-language Q&A

“What was revenue today?” or “List trial users of my badminton game” — get scoped answers instantly, no SQL required.

Write proposals, not writes

Every mutation becomes a pending action proposal. Approve it, and only then does it execute. Reject it and nothing changes.

Vendor isolation

Every query and mutation is filtered by your vendor ID from the session — never from the model. You only ever see your own data.

Revenue & growth insights

Track trials, conversions, and MRR across every title in your portfolio with clean, readable result tables.

Full audit trail

Every intent, proposal, approval, and execution is logged with correlation IDs. Rewind any decision, anytime.

Idempotent & safe

Mutations carry idempotency keys, so double-approves and retries are harmless. The agent never touches production on its own.

Architecture

Read now. Write only with your okay.

A multi-agent crew reasons over your data; a guardrail service sits between intent and execution.

  1. 1

    Ask in plain English

    Your message enters the FastAPI layer, scoped to your vendor session via JWT. Thin routes, no business logic.

  2. 2

    A crew plans the answer

    A router agent, a data-analyst agent (reads), and an action-planner agent (writes) pick typed tools — never raw SQL.

  3. 3

    The approval gate

    Reads return immediately. Writes become a pending proposal you approve or reject from the portal. Nothing executes without you.
